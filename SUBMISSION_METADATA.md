## App Description (4000 chars max)

ValuBull delivers professional-grade financial intelligence directly to your hands. Built on the same data infrastructure used by institutional analysts, ValuBull gives independent investors access to deep equity research that was once reserved for Wall Street desks.

**REAL-TIME MARKET INTELLIGENCE**
Watch prices move in real time via a live WebSocket data stream. The bioluminescent green pulse indicator confirms you're receiving institutional-grade data as it happens — not delayed snapshots from a consumer app. Price direction flips trigger tactile haptic feedback, keeping your hands informed even when your eyes are elsewhere.

**VANTA-GLASS ARCHITECTURE**
ValuBull's UI is built on a proprietary Vanta-Glass design system — a dark, glassmorphic interface inspired by the control panels of aerospace engineering. Every surface uses hardware-accelerated backdrop blur, GPU-composited animations, and native Metal rendering on Apple devices. The result is an interface that responds in milliseconds and feels like it was designed by Apple itself.

**FINANCIAL STATEMENTS IN FULL**
Access five years of audited financial statements for any publicly traded company — Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement — formatted for immediate analysis. Revenue growth, free cash flow generation, debt structure, and profitability trends are all surfaced at a glance, with no subscription paywall obscuring the data.

**DCF VALUATION ENGINE**
Run a Discounted Cash Flow analysis on any company using ValuBull's built-in valuation engine. Adjust revenue growth rates, operating margins, discount rates, and terminal multiples in real time. The sensitivity matrix displays intrinsic value across 25 parameter combinations simultaneously, letting you stress-test your assumptions before committing capital.

**KEY RATIOS & FINANCIAL HEALTH**
ValuBull's Key Ratios dashboard surfaces the metrics that matter: P/E, EV/EBITDA, Return on Equity, Debt-to-Equity, Current Ratio, Gross Margin, and more — all calculated from live financial data and benchmarked against sector averages. Color-coded heatmaps reveal financial health at a glance.

**NEURAL ALPHA SUMMARIES**
Every company page includes a Neural Alpha analysis — an AI-powered synthesis of the company's financial position, growth trajectory, and key risks. Institutional-quality research notes generated in seconds, covering what analysts care about: revenue quality, margin trends, balance sheet strength, and valuation context.

**COMPANY INTELLIGENCE LAYER**
ValuBull's Sovereign Engine pre-warms data for related companies the moment you open a ticker page. Navigate from Apple to NVIDIA to Microsoft without waiting. The stale-while-revalidate architecture means you always see data instantly — even offline — while fresh figures stream in silently behind the scenes.

**COMMUNITY CHAT**
Every ticker has a live community discussion channel. Engage with other investors, share analysis, and follow real-time sentiment around your positions. Powered by a persistent WebSocket connection, messages arrive without refreshing.

**BUILT FOR NATIVE iOS**
ValuBull is engineered for the iOS hardware it runs on. The interface is hardware-aware: the navigation glass extends behind the Dynamic Island, safe-area insets are respected everywhere, and haptic feedback is calibrated to Apple's Taptic Engine specifications. The result is an app that feels native — because it is.

**YOUR WATCHLIST, EVERYWHERE**
Save tickers to your personal watchlist and access them from any device. Watchlist data syncs across web and mobile via secure cloud authentication. Recent searches are preserved locally for instant recall.

**WHAT YOU GET**
- Live real-time price streaming via WebSocket
- Full financial statements (Income, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow) — 5 years
- DCF Valuation Engine with 25-scenario sensitivity matrix
- Key Ratios dashboard with sector benchmarking
- Neural Alpha AI-powered research summaries
- Community Chat on every ticker
- Personal Watchlist with cloud sync
- Price Chart with multiple timeframes
- Zero ads. No data selling. No paywalled core features.
